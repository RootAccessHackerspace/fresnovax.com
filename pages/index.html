Fresno County's COVID-19 Immunization Dashboard has some fantastic – and very positive! – statistics, but it doesn't show the percentage of fully vaccinated people as compared to the population that is eligible (age 16+) for the vaccine.

This statistic is vitally important because it gives us a clear idea as to how immune we are as a community, and it allows us to get back to some semblance of "normal". We built this page to track our progress in the long march to "herd immunity," which is generally thought to be around 80%.
